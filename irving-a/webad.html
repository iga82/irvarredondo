Bee Game

Description

Bee game is an android application designed to target children and help get them to exercise. The goal in the game, is to try and catch as many honey droplets that you can. There are settings to change the difficulty and speed of the game. There are four difficulty modes. On "easy", the bee only moves horizontally across the screen and can only be one color, yellow. On "normal", the bee again only moves horizontally, but now honey droplets are different colors, so the player must turn the bee different colors, by either rotating their bodies to face different directions or tapping the bee (this is also a setting in the setting menu). In "hard" and "expert" modes, the bee is no longer constrained to moving horizontally across the screen but can now move freely in an direction. The difference between the "hard" and "expert" modes is the speed. We try to accomplish getting the children to exercise by making use of the compass in the android application, although this setting can be disabled for people who have limited mobility or are feeling lazy.

Key Features

A few of our key features are:

  • Change color of bee by facing a different direction
  • Five different game modes
  • Two different speeds
  • Record of your high score, with ability to reset
  • Also support a tapping feature, for those with limited mobility
